Welcome to Our Reading Group!

The Computational Cognitive Science Reading Group is a community-driven initiative to explore recent advancements in computational models of cognition.

Scope

We cover a wide range of topics including Bayesian cognitive models, reinforcement learning, connectionist models, and cognitive architectures.

Goals

  • Stay up-to-date on cutting-edge research in cognitive science.
  • Encourage discussions bridging theory and practice.
  • Foster collaboration and idea-sharing among participants.

Audience

This group is perfect for cognitive scientists, computer scientists, psychologists, and anyone curious about computational approaches to understanding the mind.
